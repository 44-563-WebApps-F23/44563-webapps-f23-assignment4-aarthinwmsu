Aarthi Reddy Tumu acts in a play.

PLAYNAME : An Enemy of the People Act 5

AUTHOR : Henrik Ibsen

HORSTER

Oh, somehow or other. I am fairly strong, and there is more sound than fury about these folk.

Dr. STOCKMANN.

Yes, isn't their swinish cowardice astonishing? Look here, I will show you something! There are all the stones they have thrown through my windows. Just look at them! I'm hanged if there are more than two decently large bits of hard stone in the whole heap; the rest are nothing but gravel—wretched little things. And yet they stood out there bawling and swearing that they would do me some violence; but as for doing anything—you don't see much of that in this town.

HORSTER.

Just as well for you this time, doctor!
